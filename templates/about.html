About Us

Blood Donation is important for people in urgent need of it which can save millions of patients. Here Blood Donation Management System for Red Cross was developed for an easy access to all users. The main objective of this project is to create a platform where the people or patients in need of the blood can easily find a donor of the same blood group. This connects the donors and the recipients.

  • The patients in need of the blood are able to request for the blood.
  • Users can register themselves to become a donor.
  • All users are also able to see all the donors list according to different blood groups as well as the list of all the requested blood by different users or patients.
